inVision Impact Projects

We are driving change in communities by empowering people

Education

Knowledge should not be an exclusive and expensive privilege for the few. It should be available for the many. Our answer is to make education more accessible for developing countries and communities

Startups

The startup world began as an opportunity hub, but has become elitist, superficial, and, as a result, inaccessible to new blood. It is not fair for your location or even gender to stand as barriers to launching a tech startup. We are here to change that

Сreative industries

The global art and filmmaking scene is led by the most powerful markets and is not as diverse, fair, and welcoming to creators from developing countries. There is a lack of relevant education and low visibility for local artists worldwide. Our programs aim to reduce inequality in the creative industries

Sport

Sport is far more crucial yet less accessible for kids from remote areas and adults with physical disabilities. To challenge this injustice, we started global programs aimed at increasing the inclusiveness and accessibility of sports for those who need it most

inDrive challenges injustices by providing access to fair and equitable services in mobility and beyond, forging a profound impact in communities through visionary non-profit initiatives
